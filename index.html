About me

My name is Marcin and I'm Web Designer. In 2008 I graduated from University of Warmia and Mazury in Olsztyn with a Master’s degree in Technical-Information Education. For the last 6 years I've been designing websites and web applications. Recently I also started to focus my energy on front-end development projects.

I'm focusing my attention on developing my skills in creating responsive websites and web applications using HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and getting to know the most modern technologies. I take care of entrusted projects with responsibility and commitment. My professional approach helps me to meet the highest expectations.

I'm keen on team work, when people exchange solutions and ideas. Thanks to that, I acquire greater experience, and the team works faster and more efficiently. It is because of the team cooperation that it is able to accomplish even the most difficult IT tasks.
